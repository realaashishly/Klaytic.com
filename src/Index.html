Commonly asked questions

Why wouldn’t I just hire a full-time Generative AI Specialist?

Our dynamic team stays updated with the latest in Generative AI. You'll always work with the best minds in Generative AI, available when you need them. Plus you never have to pay for the full time money as well. You only pay what's required.

Is there a limit to how many requests I can have?

Once subscribed, Enjoy unlimited one-by-one work or bulk orders, delivered efficiently as they are completed.

How fast will I receive my works?

Individual orders are often delivered within a day, while bulk orders typically take a couple of days, depending on complexity.

Who are the Experts?

Klaytic boasts a team of eight top-rated individuals, collaborating seamlessly. We maintain a robust backup system with partner artists and editors to ensure we can always scale up when needed.

What programs do you work in?

We cater to various client needs using tools like Adobe Suite and advanced AI workflows to enhance results.

How do I request works?

Simply ping us on WhatsApp or Telegram with your details. We’ll set up a dedicated group to keep you updated throughout the project.

What if I don’t like the work?

Enjoy one free revision of specific parts based on feedback. Major changes, like a new script, may incur additional costs.

Is there any Gen AI work you don’t cover?

We currently do not cover custom AI workflows as it could lead to system failure and requires several days of training. Everything else is possible with Klaytic!

What if I only have a single request?

We offer custom solutions for one-off services, ensuring flexibility.

Are there any refunds if I don’t like the service?

Due to the high quality of our work, refunds are not available. Our commitment to excellence means we strive to meet and exceed your expectations every time.
